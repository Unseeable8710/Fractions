Welcome to Math!

How to use the Math extension.

Currently, there is a bug, and you can't open index.html by clicking the extension. This page tells you how to get around that.

The workaround:

Method 1:

  1. Right-click the extension.
  2. In the context menu, click "Options".
  3. Click the link that says "Go to Math!"
  4. Use Math!

Method 2:

  1. Go to chrome://extensions.
  2. Locate Math and click the "Options" button.
  3. Click the link that says "Go to Math!"
  4. Use Math!

Method 3:

  1. Go to index.html
  2. Use Math!

Well, that's it, and enjoy using Math!

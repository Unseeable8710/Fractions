Introduction: Fractions

Fractions are smaller parts of a number that can add up to the whole that it started with. Here's an example: 1/4 + 1/4 + 1/4 + 1/4 = 1 There, you're adding 4 fourths to make one whole.
Let's try another one: 1/2 + 1/2 = 1 Here, you are adding 2 halves to make one whole.

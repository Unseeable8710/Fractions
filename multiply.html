Multiplication

There are different types of multiplication. Multiplication also comes in many different forms. This page will teach you about those types and forms.

Multiplication

Multiplication is just repeated addition. For example 6 + 6 + 6 = 18. Some shorter ways to write that:

Methods
6 x 3 = 18
6 • 3 = 18
6(3) = 18
(6)3 = 18
(6)(3) = 18
6 × 3 = 18
